How Can I Help?

Beginning therapy is a meaningful step — and finding the right therapeutic relationship matters. If you are interested in working together, I invite you to reach out to schedule a consultation or inquire about services. I will respond as promptly as possible.

Please note: Standard email and web forms are not encrypted or HIPAA-secure channels. Please do not submit sensitive clinical information through this form. For anything beyond a general scheduling inquiry, please call directly.

If you are in crisis or require immediate assistance, please contact the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988, or go to your nearest emergency room.
