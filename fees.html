Transparent Pricing · Insurance · Practice Policies

Investment in Your Well-Being

Therapy is a meaningful investment in your emotional well-being, relationships, and quality of life. My practice is intentionally designed to provide highly individualized, attentive care in a private and supportive setting.

Finding the right therapeutic relationship is an important part of the healing process. I offer a complimentary 15-minute phone consultation to discuss your concerns, explore your goals, and determine whether my approach and practice are the right fit for your needs.

Out-of-Network & Superbills

Many clients with PPO insurance plans receive partial reimbursement through their out-of-network behavioral health benefits. As a courtesy, I can provide a detailed superbill that may be submitted directly to your insurance company for reimbursement.

Good Faith Estimate

Under federal law, individuals who are uninsured or choose not to use insurance have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate explaining the expected cost of services. Additional information is available upon request and will be provided prior to the start of treatment.

Your Rights Under the No Surprises Act

Under the No Surprises Act, individuals who are uninsured or choose not to use insurance have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate of expected healthcare costs. You have the right to receive a written estimate of the anticipated cost of services before beginning treatment or upon request. If you have questions about your Good Faith Estimate or your rights under this law, please contact the practice directly.

Have Questions About Fees or Insurance?

I am happy to discuss fees, insurance reimbursement, and the superbill process during your complimentary consultation. Please reach out with any questions.
